Project

This research project is part of my Ph.D. efforts, where I aim to shed light on determinants and the awareness of online media consumption. By using this application, you contribute to cutting edge scientific approaches to understand societal problems such as echo chambers.

Data security

When authorizing the app, the algorithm retrieves a list of users you follow on Twitter (which is in most cases publicly available anyway) and bases its estimation on this information. The procedure complies with the European General Data Protection Regulation (EU-GDPR) and you can find the data security form here.
